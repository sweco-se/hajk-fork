getBothDrawerAndWidgetPlugins()

A plugin has a 'target' option. Currently we use three targets: toolbar, left and right. Toolbar means it's a plugin that will be visible in Drawer list. Left and right are Widget plugins, that on large displays show on left/right side of the map viewport, while on small screens change its appearance and end up as Drawer list plugins too. This method filters out those plugins that should go into the Drawer or Widget list and returns them.
